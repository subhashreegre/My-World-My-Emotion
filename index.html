My World : My Emotion

Welcome to the creative side of me!!!
Art has always been my happy place — no matter the genre or medium, it’s where I find my calm and recharge my soul. I make it a point to dive into this therapeutic zone every day, exploring new techniques and challenging myself (because who needs formal classes when you’ve got passion, right?).
Here’s what I’m usually up to:
✨ Sculpting with clay (it’s messy, but so worth it)
✨ Drawing intricate mandalas (my kind of meditation)
✨ Sketching coasters (because functional art is the best art)
Self-taught, self-driven, and always experimenting—that’s my art story.
